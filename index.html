The Author

About Ben Luxon

Drawing inspiration from technology, philosophy, and the art of world-building, Ben Luxon crafts immersive stories that challenge perspectives and ignite the imagination.

His debut release, Sunset in the East, is a compelling collection of mind-bending sci-fi short stories, blending psychological thrillers with humorous absurdism.

When not writing, Ben writes about writing — sharing hard-won lessons on craft, structure, and the long road from first draft to published book.
