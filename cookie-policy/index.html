What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to the site owner.

How we use cookies

benluxonauthor.com uses a minimal set of cookies to ensure the site functions correctly and to remember your preferences. We do not use cookies for advertising or to track you across other websites.
